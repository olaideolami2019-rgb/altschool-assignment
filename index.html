Jollof Rice Recipe     

Jollof Rice

          

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, and spices. it is flavorful and loved across many countries.

    

Recipe Details

    
            
  • Preparation Time: 30 minutes
    •         
  • Cooking Time: 45 minutes
    •         
  • Servings: 4-6 people
    •         
  • Difficulty Level:Beginner
    •     
    

Ingredients

                
                        
  • 5 cups long-grain rice
    •                     
  • 1 can (400g) diced tomatoes
    •                     
  • 2 onion, chopped
    •                     
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    •                     
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
    •                     
  • 1 teaspoon thyme
    •                     
  • 1 teaspoon curry powder
    •                     
  • Salt and pepper to taste
    •                     
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
    •                 
    

Instructions

    
            
  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear. Set aside.
    2.         
  2. In a large pot, heat the vegetable oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and garlic, and sauté until they are soft and fragrant.
    3.         
  3. Add the diced tomatoes and bell pepper to the pot. Cook for about 5 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
    4.         
  4. Stir in the thyme, curry powder, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 2 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
    5.         
  5. Add the rinsed rice to the pot and stir well to coat the rice with the tomato mixture.
    6.         
  6. Pour in 4 cups of water and bring to a boil. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and let it simmer for about 20-25 minutes or until the rice is cooked and has absorbed all the liquid.
    7.         
  7. Remove from heat and let it sit for a few minutes before serving.
