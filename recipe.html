Jollof Rice

By chef salam

Jollof rice is a popular West African dish made with rice, tomatoes, and spices. It is flavorful, colorful, and a party favorite across Nigeria.

Recipe Details

  • Prep Time: 15 minutes
  • Cook Time: 45 minutes
  • Servings: 6 people
  • Difficulty: Medium

Ingredients

  • 3 cups long grain parboiled rice
  • 1 cup tomato paste
  • 2 large onions, chopped
  • 3 red bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 teaspoons thyme
  • 2 teaspoons curry powder
  • Salt to taste

Instructions

  1. Wash rice thoroughly and parboil for 5 minutes. Drain
  2. Blend tomatoes, peppers, and 1 onion into a smooth paste
  3. Heat oil in a large pot. Fry chopped onions until translucent
  4. Add tomato paste and blended mixture. Cook for 15 minutes
  5. Add thyme, curry, and salt. Stir in parboiled rice.
  6. Add 4 cups of water, cover tightly, and cook on low heat

Tips & Notes

  • Use a heavy-bottom pot to prevent burning.
  • For smoky "party jollof" flavor, let the bottom layer burn slightly.
  • Serve with fried plantain and grilled chicken.
